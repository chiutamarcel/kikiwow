Rules

  1. Respectful Behavior: Treat all players, staff, and the server with respect. Discrimination, harassment, or any form of disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.
  2. No Cheating or Exploiting: Any form of cheating, hacking, or exploiting game mechanics is strictly prohibited. This includes but is not limited to using third-party software, exploiting glitches, or abusing bugs.
  3. Account Security: Players are responsible for the security of their accounts. Sharing accounts or account information is not allowed. Use strong, unique passwords and enable additional security measures whenever possible.
  4. Language and Communication: Keep chat and communication civil and appropriate. Avoid offensive language, spamming, or excessive use of caps lock. Any form of advertising, including links to external websites, is not permitted without permission.
  5. No Scamming or Fraud: Engaging in scamming, fraudulent activities, or any form of deception is strictly prohibited. This includes scamming players in trades, auctions, or any other interactions.
  6. Respect Game Masters: Follow instructions given by Game Masters (GMs) and staff members. Disrespecting or harassing GMs will result in disciplinary action.
  7. Privacy and Safety: Do not share personal information about yourself or others. This includes real names, addresses, phone numbers, or any other identifying information.
  8. In-Game Conduct: Behave in a manner that enhances the gaming experience for all players. This includes avoiding griefing, ninja looting, or intentionally disrupting gameplay.
  9. Reporting Bugs and Issues: Report any bugs, exploits, or issues encountered in the game to the staff promptly. Do not abuse or publicize known bugs or exploits.
  10. Fair Play: Play the game fairly and within the spirit of fair competition. This includes refraining from win-trading, collusion, or any other form of unfair advantage.
  11. Respect Server Policies: Familiarize yourself with and abide by all server policies, including terms of service and rules specific to the server.
  12. Consequences for Violations: Violating any of these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the server, depending on the severity of the offense.
